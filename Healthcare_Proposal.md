# AI/ML Proposal for Healthcare Industry

## Top AI/ML Use Cases
1. **Clinical Decision Support**: Helps clinicians with intelligent decision-making by analyzing medical data.
2. **Predictive Analytics for Operations**: Optimizes hospital resource allocation and improves operational efficiency.
3. **Drug Discovery**: Reduces research time and costs by using AI for data analysis in drug discovery.
4. **Chatbots and Virtual Health Assistants**: Improves customer service and reduces wait times with AI-powered assistants.
5. **Radiography and Imaging Analysis**: Supports diagnostic accuracy by analyzing medical images through AI.
